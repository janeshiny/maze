Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing accurate and efficient detection of diseases in tea leaves. Our goal is to help tea farmers identify and address diseases early, thus promoting healthy tea plantations and higher yields.

Disease Prediction

Using advanced machine learning algorithms and image processing techniques, we analyze tea leaf images to identify disease symptoms. Our model can predict various tea leaf diseases with high accuracy, allowing farmers to take timely actions to protect their crops.
